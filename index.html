About Me

I am a young and passionate designer with a keenness for building games on the foundation of uniqueness, detail, and visual appeal. With 4 dedicated years of hands-on experience, I have honed my skills in crafting immersive gaming experiences that resonate with players on both an emotional and intellectual level.


My journey in game design began at a young age when my leisure time was spent playing a variety of computer games on my dad's work computer. I was fascinated by the idea that a game so fun and immersive came from nothing but an idea in someone's head. This led me to pursue the industry of game design to better understand how great games are made. Now, my goal is to recreate my childhood fascination with games for the next generation.


I thrive on the collaborative nature of game development, relishing the opportunity to work alongside talented artists, programmers, and writers to bring ideas to life. Whether it's brainstorming game mechanics, fine-tuning level design, or shaping narrative arcs, I approach each aspect of game development with enthusiasm and a commitment to excellence.
